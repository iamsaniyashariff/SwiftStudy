About Us

We are two students currently pursuing our undergrad who want to make learning an easier experience for everyone.

We aim to build an adaptive learning platform for students of all ages and study levels. This website employs a multi-faceted approach to enhance learning, incorporating techniques such as flashcards and similar interactive resources to help optimize the learning experience, making it more engaging and efficient for users.

The students will be able to create their own notebooks as well and will receive personalized learning. This platform aims to efficiently capture and distill key information, providing the text in a brief and streamlined manner.

As college students ourselves, we are firsthand experiencing the shift from textbooks to online resources. To make it easier for ourselves and our fellow students, we truly think that this website will be a lifesaver.
